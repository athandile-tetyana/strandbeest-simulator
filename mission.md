# Mission — Strandbeest Simulator

## Problem
Most people have no idea how Strandbeest walking mechanisms actually work. 
There's no accessible interactive tool to experiment with leg geometry and physics.

## Solution
A browser-based sandbox where users drag-and-drop joint positions to design
walking mechanisms, simulate them with real physics, and share their creations.

## Target User
Curious builders, makers, Hack Club teens, and anyone who watched a Strandbeest video and thought "how does that work?"

## Success Criteria
- [ ] User can place and connect joints to build a leg mechanism
- [ ] Physics simulation runs in real time (Matter.js or Rapier)
- [ ] User can save and share their design via URL or Supabase
- [ ] Works fully in browser with no setup required
- [ ] 40+ hours logged via Hackatime

## Why This Wins (Beest)
Directly on theme. Visually stunning. No one else is building this. 
The submission story writes itself: "I simulated Strandbeests before I ever saw one."
